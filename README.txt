Predicting Phosphorous Levels in Chicago MWRD Plant Inflows
Final Project for CS584 Fall 2017, Prof. Wai Gen Yee, Illinois Institute of Technology

The consolidated dataset is in 'ALL_MWRD.csv' in the data directory. The raw datasets we consolidated from are in the data/infl directory. Output plots and regression output are in the data/plots and data/results directories.

The scripts used to perform analysis are in src/infl_mod.R.
